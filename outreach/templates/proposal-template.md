## Executive Summary

**The Challenge:**
[In 2-3 sentences, summarize what they told you in the discovery call. Their pain point, what's not working, urgency.]

Example:
> "Your product team doubled after your Series A, but shipping velocity slowed by 50%. You're burning through runway without the growth your investors expect, and the team is frustrated with unclear priorities and missed deadlines."

**The Solution:**
[In 2-3 sentences, what you'll do to fix it.]

Example:
> "I'll embed with your team as Fractional CPO for 3 months, install a predictable delivery process (Shape Up framework), and restore 6-week shipping cycles. You'll have a working system your team can run independently after I hand it off."

**The Outcome:**
[What they'll have at the end.]

Example:
> "Predictable delivery, clear roadmap for next 12 weeks, confident team that ships without chaos, and documented process you own and operate."
